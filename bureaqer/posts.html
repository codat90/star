Magnate Dudong found dead

The celebutante Dudong Taba has been found lifeless at Idlewood, Los Santos, with a mysterious setup surrounding his death. The, yet to be known murderer left behind a cross, a shovel, a stuffed parrot, and what seems to be as a big pile of raw meat. These tools and leftover flesh might be a sign of signature modus operandi from a new serial killer in Los Santos.
