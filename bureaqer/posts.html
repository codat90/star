Sheriff Department Hosting a BBQ

The San Andreas Sheriff's Department has announced that it will hold an hour-long BBQ party at 4pm on August 7. You can contact Sheriff Daniel Steele (@'Wes#6563) to have a stand area at the BBQ party that will take place at Palomino Creek. The San Andreas Sheriff's Department also announced that it will hold a boat race. It has been announced that a cash prize will be given to those who will enter the top 3 of the race. The amount of the prize money is unknown.
